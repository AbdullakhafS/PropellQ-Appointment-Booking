## 1. Purpose

This policy defines the minimum retention period, archival lifecycle, and deletion controls for all audit log records generated by the PropelIQ Appointment Booking platform.  It satisfies the HIPAA requirement to retain audit activity documentation for a minimum of 6 years (45 CFR ┬¦ 164.530(j)); PropelIQ adopts a stricter 7-year minimum to align with state-level healthcare retention laws.

---

## 2. Scope

Applies to all records stored in the `audit_log` storage namespace (distinct from the transactional `transactional` namespace).  Records include every `AuditEntry` appended via `AppendOnlyAuditStore`.

---

## 3. Retention Period

| Tier | Duration | Storage Class | Retrieval SLA |

| **Active** | 0 ŌĆō 7 years from entry timestamp | Primary (hot) store | < 1 s |
| **Archive** | After 7 years, retained indefinitely until approved deletion | Archive (cold) tier | Ōēż 24 h |

- **Minimum retention:** 7 years (2 555 days, conservative no-leap calculation).
- No record may be moved out of the active tier before the 7-year mark.
- Archived records remain retrievable on demand; they are never silently purged.

## 5. Deletion Criteria

A record is eligible for **permanent deletion** only when **all** conditions are met:

| Condition | Enforcement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Record timestamp is more than 7 years old | `AuditRetentionPolicy.is_eligible_for_deletion()` |
| Record has been moved to the Archive Tier | `AuditArchiveTier` membership check |
| Deletion request carries valid `DeletionApproval` (approver ID + reason) | `AuditedDeletionController.request_deletion()` |

Records that fail any condition are rejected and the attempt is logged as a `LIFECYCLE_DELETE_DENIED` event.

---

## 6. Controls and Evidence

- **Append-only enforcement:** `AppendOnlyAuditStore.delete()` / `update()` raise `AuditImmutabilityError` for active records.
- **HMAC hash chain:** every entry carries an `integrity_hash` over content + preceding hash; tamper evidence is provided by `AuditIntegrityChecker`.
- **Deletion audit trail:** every deletion (approved or denied) is logged as an immutable `AuditEntry` before any storage mutation occurs.
- **Compliance report:** `generate_retention_compliance_report()` produces structured evidence covering policy settings, archival statistics, and integrity verification results.
