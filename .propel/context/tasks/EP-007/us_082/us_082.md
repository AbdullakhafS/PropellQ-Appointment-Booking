# US-082: Establish Vendor BAA Process

**Story ID:** US-082  
**Epic:** EP-007 (Compliance & Audit Logging)  
**Priority:** HIGH  
**Story Points:** 3  
**Owner Role:** Compliance / Legal  
**Status:** Draft

## User Story

As a **compliance officer**, I want a formal vendor Business Associate Agreement (BAA) process so that third-party providers handling PHI are contractually bound to HIPAA safeguards.

## Business Value

- Ensures vendor compliance with HIPAA requirements
- Protects the organization from third-party liability
- Provides a contractual framework for secure data handling

## Scope

In scope:
- Vendor selection and due diligence checklist for BAAs
- BAA contract template and approval workflow
- Process for ongoing vendor compliance review

Out of scope:
- Negotiation of specific vendor contract terms
- Vendor operational onboarding beyond compliance process

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a third-party vendor, then the BAA process includes due diligence and risk assessment steps.
2. Given a vendor relationship, then a BAA template is available and reviewed by legal/compliance.
3. Given a vendor contract is signed, then the process includes periodic compliance review triggers.
4. Given changes in vendor scope, then BAA status and documentation are updated.

## Technical Notes

- Include checklist items for data access, encryption, breach notification, and audit rights.
- Align vendor BAA process with overall HIPAA compliance program.

## Dependencies

- Legal and compliance stakeholder input.
- Vendor management process and contract repository.

## Definition of Done

- Vendor BAA process documented and approved.
- BAA template available for vendor engagements.
- Compliance review confirms process coverage.
