# US-076: Implement Audit Log Retention (7 years)

**Story ID:** US-076  
**Epic:** EP-007 (Compliance & Audit Logging)  
**Priority:** CRITICAL  
**Story Points:** 5  
**Owner Role:** Security / Operations Engineer  
**Status:** Draft

## User Story

As a **compliance officer**, I want audit logs retained for at least 7 years so that the organization can meet HIPAA retention requirements and support long-term investigations.

## Business Value

- Ensures regulatory retention compliance
- Preserves audit history for long-term investigations
- Reduces legal and compliance risk

## Scope

In scope:
- Audit log retention policy configured for 7 years
- Archival and retrieval mechanisms for old logs
- Automated retention enforcement and deletion controls

Out of scope:
- Analytics on historical audit data beyond retrieval and access

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given an audit log entry, then it is retained for a minimum of 7 years before deletion.
2. Given an archival request, then logs older than the active retention window remain available for retrieval.
3. Given retention rules are applied, then only expired records are eligible for deletion.
4. Given a compliance audit, then retention policy and evidence of enforcement are documented.
5. Given any deletion operation, then it is logged and subject to approval controls.

## Technical Notes

- Use lifecycle policies or archival tiers in log storage.
- Ensure deletion controls cannot remove logs before retention expiration.
- Plan for storage growth and cost management.

## Dependencies

- Immutable audit log infrastructure from US-074.
- Storage and backup policies from infrastructure.

## Definition of Done

- 7-year retention policy enforced.
- Archival/retrieval process validated.
- Deletion controls audited.
- Compliance documentation updated.
