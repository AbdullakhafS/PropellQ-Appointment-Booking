# US-037: Implement Patient Check-In Workflow

**Story ID:** US-037  
**Epic:** EP-004 (Appointment Operations & Queue Management)  
**Priority:** HIGH  
**Story Points:** 4  
**Owner Role:** Product / Platform Engineer  
**Status:** Draft

## User Story

As a **front-desk staff member**, I want a patient check-in workflow that updates appointment status and records arrival time so that attendance is tracked accurately and patients are routed correctly.

## Business Value

- Ensures accurate visit tracking and attendance data
- Keeps clinical teams informed of patient arrivals
- Supports patient flow management and waitlist processing

## Scope

In scope:
- Check-in action for same-day and walk-in appointments
- Appointment status transitions such as "Scheduled", "Arrived", and "In Progress"
- Visible confirmation of patient arrival in the queue

Out of scope:
- Clinical encounter start/stop workflows
- Billing check-out processing

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a staff user selects an appointment, then they can mark the patient as checked in.
2. Given the patient is checked in, then the appointment status updates to "Arrived" and the arrival timestamp is recorded.
3. Given the queue displays appointment status, then checked-in patients appear with arrival time and updated status.
4. Given a patient is checked in, then related waitlist offers or slot allocations update accordingly.

## Technical Notes

- Use an appointment status field with discrete workflow states.
- Record arrival timestamp for each check-in event.
- Ensure check-in updates are broadcast to queue listeners.

## Dependencies

- Appointment state management APIs
- Real-time queue updates from US-034
- Staff permissions from EP-005

## Definition of Done

- Check-in workflow implemented and testable
- Arrival timestamp persisted with appointment status
- Queue reflects checked-in patients accurately
- Acceptance criteria validated with sample scenarios
