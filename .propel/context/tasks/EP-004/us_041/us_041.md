# US-041: Implement Cancellation/Reschedule Logic

**Story ID:** US-041  
**Epic:** EP-004 (Appointment Operations & Queue Management)  
**Priority:** HIGH  
**Story Points:** 4  
**Owner Role:** Product / Platform Engineer  
**Status:** Draft

## User Story

As a **staff scheduler**, I want cancellations and reschedules to update the appointment queue and notify affected patients so that the staff workflow stays synchronized and open slots are handled efficiently.

## Business Value

- Reduces wasted capacity from last-minute cancellations
- Keeps staff and patients aligned on schedule changes
- Supports automated waitlist filling and queue updates

## Scope

In scope:
- Staff-initiated appointment cancellation and rescheduling
- Queue and schedule updates when appointments change
- Notification of affected patients and staff

Out of scope:
- Patient self-service cancellation/reschedule flows (EP-001)
- Insurance or billing impact of cancellations

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a staff user cancels an appointment, then the appointment status updates and the queue refreshes.
2. Given a staff user reschedules an appointment, then the new slot is validated and saved.
3. Given an appointment is canceled or rescheduled, then any dependent waitlist offers are re-evaluated.
4. Given staff cancel or reschedule, then notification is sent to the affected patient and assigned provider.

## Technical Notes

- Use appointment state transitions for cancellations and reschedules.
- Ensure reschedules preserve patient and provider context.
- Trigger waitlist processing when slots are freed.

## Dependencies

- Appointment queue and waitlist services
- Notification service
- Staff authorization from EP-005

## Definition of Done

- Cancellation and reschedule logic implemented
- Queue updates reflected immediately
- Notifications delivered for affected appointments
- Acceptance criteria validated with workflow tests
