# US-039: Implement Waitlist Join & Accept/Decline Flow

**Story ID:** US-039  
**Epic:** EP-004 (Appointment Operations & Queue Management)  
**Priority:** HIGH  
**Story Points:** 5  
**Owner Role:** Product / Platform Engineer  
**Status:** Draft

## User Story

As a **staff user**, I want patients to be able to join a waitlist and receive an accept/decline offer when a slot opens so that the clinic can fill capacity and patients have a clear choice.

## Business Value

- Maximizes same-day capacity utilization
- Reduces lost revenue from empty slots
- Improves patient satisfaction with explicit waitlist offers

## Scope

In scope:
- Patient waitlist join flow for full time slots
- Waitlist order management with FIFO and priority adjustments
- Offer notifications when a slot becomes available
- Accept and decline responses with expiration window

Out of scope:
- Patient-facing waitlist mobile app
- Automated priority scoring beyond basic rules

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given an appointment slot is full, then staff can add a patient to the waitlist.
2. Given a slot opens, then the first eligible waitlisted patient receives an offer.
3. Given a patient receives an offer, then they can accept or decline it within 30 minutes.
4. Given the patient accepts, then the system converts the waitlist entry to a booked appointment.
5. Given the patient declines or times out, then the next waitlisted patient receives an offer.

## Technical Notes

- Model waitlist entries separately from scheduled appointments.
- Store offer expiry timestamps and response states.
- Notify waitlisted patients through configured channels.

## Dependencies

- Appointment scheduling engine
- Notification delivery from EP-001
- Staff and patient permissions from EP-005

## Definition of Done

- Waitlist join and offer flow implemented
- Accept/decline behavior recorded with timeouts
- Transition from waitlist entry to appointment occurs correctly
- Acceptance criteria validated with scenario tests
