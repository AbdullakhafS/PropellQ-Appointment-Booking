**Status:** Planned

## Objective
Produce an operations-approved disaster recovery plan covering infrastructure, data, application, and cache recovery with defined RTO/RPO, roles, and step-by-step recovery procedures.

## AC Mapping
- AC-1: DOC-1, QA-1
- AC-2: DOC-2, QA-2
- AC-3: DOC-3, QA-3
- AC-4: DOC-4, QA-4

## Tasks
### DOC-1: Recovery Procedure Authoring
- Document step-by-step recovery guidance for major outage scenarios.

### DOC-2: Recovery Test and Lessons Section
- Include drill usage notes and a section for lessons learned capture.

### DOC-3: RTO/RPO Definition
- Define recovery objectives and acceptance thresholds by critical service.

### DOC-4: Stakeholder Review and Approval
- Route plan for ops stakeholder review and approval.

### QA-1: Procedure Completeness Review
- Validate plan covers infra, data, app, and cache layers.

### QA-2: Drill Readiness Review
- Validate plan is usable during a controlled recovery test.

### QA-3: Objective Review
- Validate RTO/RPO are explicit and measurable.

### QA-4: Approval Review
- Validate stakeholder sign-off captured.

## Definition of Done
- [ ] Disaster recovery plan documented.
- [ ] RTO/RPO defined.
- [ ] Stakeholder review completed.
- [ ] AC-1 through AC-4 validated.
