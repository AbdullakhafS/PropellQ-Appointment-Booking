# US-096: Document Disaster Recovery Plan

**Story ID:** US-096  
**Epic:** EP-008 (System Reliability & Performance)  
**Priority:** HIGH  
**Story Points:** 3  
**Owner Role:** SRE / Operations Engineer  
**Status:** Draft

## User Story

As a **reliability engineer**, I want a documented disaster recovery plan so that the team can restore operations quickly after a major outage.

## Business Value

- Provides a clear recovery path for outages
- Reduces recovery time and operational confusion
- Supports business continuity planning

## Scope

In scope:
- Recovery procedures for infrastructure, data, and application services
- Roles and responsibilities during recovery
- Recovery objectives and acceptance criteria

Out of scope:
- Detailed incident response playbooks for every failure mode

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a major outage, then the disaster recovery plan provides step-by-step recovery guidance.
2. Given a recovery test, then the plan is used and lessons are captured.
3. Given a disaster recovery plan, then recovery objectives (RTO/RPO) are defined.
4. Given the plan is reviewed, then it is approved by operations stakeholders.

## Technical Notes

- Include recovery for load balancer, database, application, and cache layers.
- Define communication and escalation procedures.
- Identify critical dependencies and backup restoration steps.

## Dependencies

- Infrastructure topology and service inventory.
- Backup and restore capabilities from EP-DATA-001.

## Definition of Done

- Disaster recovery plan documented.
- Recovery objectives defined.
- Plan reviewed and approved.
