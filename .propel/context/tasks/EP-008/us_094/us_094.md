# US-094: Implement Uptime Monitoring & Alerting

**Story ID:** US-094  
**Epic:** EP-008 (System Reliability & Performance)  
**Priority:** HIGH  
**Story Points:** 5  
**Owner Role:** SRE / Monitoring Engineer  
**Status:** Draft

## User Story

As a **monitoring engineer**, I want uptime monitoring and alerting so that we can detect service outages quickly and maintain the 99.9% availability SLA.

## Business Value

- Enables rapid detection of outages and incidents
- Supports SLA compliance and operational visibility
- Reduces mean time to detection and recovery

## Scope

In scope:
- Uptime probes for API and web services
- Alerting for service unavailability and threshold breaches
- Dashboards for availability metrics and incident status

Out of scope:
- Full observability of business metrics unrelated to uptime

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given service availability probes, then uptime is monitored continuously.
2. Given a service outage or degraded state, then alerts are sent to the operations team.
3. Given uptime metrics, then dashboards display availability and recent incident history.
4. Given alert thresholds, then they are configurable and documented.

## Technical Notes

- Use synthetic monitoring and internal probes.
- Track uptime percentage and SLA violations over time.
- Integrate with existing incident management tools if available.

## Dependencies

- Health checks and load balancer configuration.
- Monitoring/alerting platform.

## Definition of Done

- Uptime monitoring configured and tested.
- Alerts and dashboards implemented.
- SLA metrics captured and documented.
