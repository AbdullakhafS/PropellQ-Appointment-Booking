# Task 001: Conduct User Research & Intake Interview Study

**Task ID:** task_012_001  
**User Story:** US-012 (Design Multi-Step Chatbot Conversation Flow)  
**Epic:** EP-002  
**Priority:** HIGH  
**Story Points:** 2  
**Owner Role:** Product / UX Researcher  
**Status:** Not Started

## Objective

Conduct qualitative user research with 8-10 patients to understand intake preferences, pain points, and communication style preferences that inform conversation flow design.

## Requirements

1. **Research Plan**
   - Define recruitment criteria: diverse age, gender, health literacy, tech comfort
   - Identify 8-10 patient participants (or use existing patient interviews)
   - Create interview guide with open-ended questions

2. **Interview Topics**
   - Current intake experience: What's difficult? What's easy?
   - Preferred question order: "Would you prefer medical history or medications first?"
   - Tone preference: "Should chatbot be formal or conversational?"
   - Communication style: "How do you prefer to be asked about health?"
   - Question clarity: "Which phrasings are clearest? 'Drug allergies' vs. 'Medication allergies'?"
   - Pain points: "What makes you frustrated with forms?"
   - Time expectations: "How long do you expect intake to take?"

3. **Data Collection**
   - Record interviews (with consent)
   - Take detailed notes on key quotes and themes
   - Capture participant demographics and health literacy level
   - Document any usability observations

4. **Analysis**
   - Thematic coding: Identify common patterns and pain points
   - Sentiment analysis: Positive/negative feedback on current intake
   - Create user personas or archetypes (based on interviews)
   - Prioritize insights by frequency and impact

5. **Findings Report**
   - Summary of key insights
   - Recommended conversation flow principles based on research
   - Pain points to address in chatbot design
   - Questions/phrasing that resonated with patients

## Implementation Steps

1. Create recruitment criteria and reach out to participants
2. Develop interview guide with key topics
3. Schedule and conduct 8-10 interviews (60 min each)
4. Transcribe or summarize interviews
5. Code responses and identify themes
6. Create summary report with key findings and recommendations
7. Share findings with design and product team

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] 8-10 patient interviews completed and documented
- [ ] Interview recordings/transcripts stored securely
- [ ] Thematic analysis completed with key patterns identified
- [ ] User personas or archetypes defined
- [ ] Key insights and recommendations documented
- [ ] Top 3-5 pain points identified and prioritized
- [ ] Preferred question phrasing/tone recommendations provided
- [ ] Findings reviewed with product team

## Dependencies

- Recruitment channels available (clinic, online panels, or existing patient database)
- Patient consent and privacy protocols in place
- IRB approval if required (healthcare research)

## Validation

- [ ] Interviews cover diverse patient population
- [ ] Themes and patterns clearly documented
- [ ] Findings directly inform conversation flow design
- [ ] Product team agrees with key recommendations

## Definition of Done

- Research report completed and approved
- Findings shared with design and product teams
- Key insights used to inform task_012_002 (flow design)
- Recordings securely archived and accessible
