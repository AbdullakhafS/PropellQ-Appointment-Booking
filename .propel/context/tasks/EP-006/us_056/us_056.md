# US-056: Display Personal Health Profile

**Story ID:** US-056  
**Epic:** EP-006 (Patient Portal & Analytics)  
**Priority:** HIGH  
**Story Points:** 4  
**Owner Role:** Product / Platform Engineer  
**Status:** Draft

## User Story

As a **patient**, I want to see my personal health profile details so that I can verify my medications, allergies, and diagnoses are accurate.

## Business Value

- Improves patient trust in clinical data accuracy
- Enables easier correction of profile discrepancies
- Supports safer care by surfacing critical health details

## Scope

In scope:
- Display of current medications, allergies, diagnoses, and chronic conditions
- Summary of active care plans and clinical alerts
- Patient-facing health profile section within the portal

Out of scope:
- Editing clinical data directly by the patient
- Clinical decision support tools

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a patient views their profile, then current medication, allergy, and diagnosis summaries are shown.
2. Given the patient identifies an issue, then there is a clear path for reporting corrections.
3. Given the patient loads the profile, then the data appears in a readable, patient-friendly format.
4. Given the source data updates, then the profile refreshes accordingly.

## Technical Notes

- Consume structured profile data from the clinical intelligence platform.
- Use readable labels and avoid medical jargon where possible.
- Include data quality notice for approximate or pending items.

## Dependencies

- Health profile aggregation from EP-003
- Patient portal authentication from EP-005

## Definition of Done

- Personal health profile section implemented
- Data sections render clearly and accurately
- Corrections or support actions are surfaced for patients
- Acceptance criteria validated with representative data
