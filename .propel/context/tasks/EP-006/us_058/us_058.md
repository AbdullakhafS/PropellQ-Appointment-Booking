# US-058: Enable Notification Preference Management

**Story ID:** US-058  
**Epic:** EP-006 (Patient Portal & Analytics)  
**Priority:** MEDIUM  
**Story Points:** 3  
**Owner Role:** Product / Platform Engineer  
**Status:** Draft

## User Story

As a **patient**, I want to manage my notification preferences so that I can choose whether to receive appointment reminders by email, SMS, or app notificatio ns.

## Business Value

- Reduces unwanted communications and improves consent management
- Increases patient satisfaction with personalized notifications
- Supports compliance with communication preferences

## Scope

In scope:
- Preference settings for email, SMS, and in-app notifications
- Opt-in/opt-out controls on the patient dashboard
- Save and persist preference changes

Out of scope:
- Delivery channel implementation for every notification type
- Push notification platform setup

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a patient opens preferences, then they can select their preferred notification channels.
2. Given the patient saves preferences, then changes persist and apply to future reminders.
3. Given the patient opts out of a channel, then notifications for that channel are suppressed.
4. Given the patient revisits preferences, then current settings are displayed correctly.

## Technical Notes

- Persist preferences in the patient profile or notification settings store.
- Validate email and mobile number formats as needed.
- Avoid sending notifications to opted-out channels.

## Dependencies

- Notification settings backend service
- Patient profile and contact info APIs

## Definition of Done

- Notification preference management UI implemented
- Preference changes persist and load correctly
- Opt-out behavior reflected in notification settings
- Acceptance criteria verified with sample patient accounts
