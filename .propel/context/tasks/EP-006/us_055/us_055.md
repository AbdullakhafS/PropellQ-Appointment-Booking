# US-055: Display Past Appointments with Clinical Notes

**Story ID:** US-055  
**Epic:** EP-006 (Patient Portal & Analytics)  
**Priority:** MEDIUM  
**Story Points:** 3  
**Owner Role:** Product / UX Engineer  
**Status:** Draft

## User Story

As a **patient**, I want to view my past appointments and released clinical notes so that I can review my care history and follow-up instructions.

## Business Value

- Improves patient understanding of their care journey
- Increases transparency and continuity of care
- Reduces repeat questions to clinic staff

## Scope

In scope:
- Past appointment history list with date, provider, and status
- Display of clinical notes or visit summary when available
- Link to downloaded documents or visit summaries

Out of scope:
- Full electronic health record access
- Unreleased or restricted clinical content

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a patient has past visits, then those appointments appear in a historical list.
2. Given clinical notes are released for a visit, then the patient can view or download them.
3. Given a visit has no released notes, then a clear message indicates the content is unavailable.
4. Given the patient selects a past appointment, then details load without errors.

## Technical Notes

- Respect release policies for clinical notes and PHI visibility.
- Use secure links for downloadable visit summaries.
- Provide a consistent list view with concise metadata.

## Dependencies

- Clinical intelligence and document release data from EP-003
- Patient authentication from EP-005

## Definition of Done

- Past appointments display correctly on the patient portal
- Released notes surface for eligible visits
- UX handles missing note availability gracefully
- Acceptance criteria verified with sample patient history
